Our Approach

Identity in a Remote-First, Agentic World

Traditional IDV checks a document at one moment in time. That's not enough anymore.

The Old Way
  • Single checkpoint (ID scan at onboarding)
  • One signal (document verification)
  • Point-in-time, then trust forever
  • Works for in-person, fails for remote
The Clarity Way
  • Continuous identity signals across the lifecycle
  • Multiple signals combined: CV analysis, deepfake detection, biometric matching, behavioral context, document verification
  • Each signal alone is a commodity — the orchestration is the moat
  • Built for remote-first, distributed, agentic workforce

Any single signal can be commoditized. Orchestrating them across the enterprise — that's what's hard. That's what we do.
